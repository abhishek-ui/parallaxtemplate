ABOUT US

WHO ARE WE ?

Think of us as a group of Entrepreneurs, IITians, Designers and MBAs who love solving consumer problems by using technology. We are a part of Times Internet Limited (TIL), India's largest digital products company, owned by the Times of India group. Times Internet is the maker of great digital consumer brands like TimesofIndia.com, EconomicTimes.com, Indiatimes.com, gaana.com, magicbricks.com and many more. More than 10 Crore Indians engage with these brands every month!


WHAT KEEPS US EXCITED ?

There are 3 core beliefs that keep us super excited at Smartspends -
1. Everything related to money until now has been complex, super boring, scary and big daddy-like. At Smartspends, we are on a mission to change this by offering you a refreshingly new way to stay on top of your spends and help you get smarter on every purchase you make.
2. Personal finance industry has remained largely unchanged for more than 20 years and has not caught up with the changing needs of today's smart consumers like you. We see a big opportunity to change this status quo by reimagining everything related to money with mobile at its center.
3. More importantly, helping people get smarter with spends in a fun and easy manner is bound to bring a lot of joy to people and that brings a lot of joy to us in return :)
